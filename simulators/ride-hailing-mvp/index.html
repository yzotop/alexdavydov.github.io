How to read this
  • Orders appear in zones according to the demand pattern; each order has a pickup and dropoff.
  • Matching runs in batches; it assigns idle drivers to waiting orders using a spatial grid (fast nearest search).
  • Surge is computed per zone from pending demand / idle supply, then smoothed by EMA.
  • Cancellations happen while waiting or before pickup, increasing with pickup ETA vs reference ETA0.
  • Driver colors: idle (gray), to pickup (blue), to dropoff (green). Orders: waiting (dot), assigned (ring).
  • Metrics on the right are rolling over the last 60 seconds; charts show the last 120 seconds.
  • If demand overwhelms supply, pending orders can grow. We cap active orders to keep the sim stable.
  • Use seed for reproducible runs. Randomize changes the whole stochastic trajectory.
