Waiting Time

When something must happen, but not now.

Knowing that a transition will occur does not tell you when.

A system may follow stable rules and still delay a transition. Each step carries the same probability, yet the waiting time is unpredictable. This uncertainty is a property of the dynamics.

Long waiting times are not anomalies. They are compatible with stable rules. Silence does not imply safety.
