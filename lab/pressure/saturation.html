Saturation

More pressure, less response.

Beyond a point, added force no longer moves the system.

Saturation is a limit of influence. Early pressure changes the distribution. Later pressure mostly increases stress, not movement. The system becomes harder to move — and easier to break.

When the response saturates, optimizing harder can backfire. The signal stops improving. The risk keeps rising.
