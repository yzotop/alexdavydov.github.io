Geometry of Uncertainty

How possibility space bends.

Uncertainty is not noise. It is the space of futures a system can still reach.

Decisions and constraints reshape that space before outcomes change. Geometry is the language of what becomes possible — and what collapses.

Outcomes change later. Option spaces collapse immediately.

Option Space Collapse
Commitment reshapes the future.
A decision can collapse reachable futures before any metric moves.
Uncertainty Surface
Where the system is sensitive.
Some regions amplify small changes. Others absorb them.
Curvature of Decisions
Small choices, large bends.
Tiny actions can strongly deform long-run trajectories.
Feasible Set
Constraints define the world.
What you can't do matters as much as what you can.
Reachability vs Optimality
The best solution you cannot reach is irrelevant.
Optimization assumes access. Reality imposes reachability.

Markov lens explains motion. Geometry explains what remains reachable.

Uncertainty has shape.
